About

Bridging the gap between
classroom and market

Founded in 2019 at Reichman University, Investment Group Israel (IGI) is a student-led professional club within the Arison School of Business dedicated to bridging the gap between academic theory and practical market execution. IGI provides students from all academic backgrounds with the opportunity to study the global investment landscape at an institutional level.

Members engage directly with high-level practitioners — hedge fund managers, activist investors, and venture capital partners — through private forums and strategic sessions. The academic year culminates in a Year-End Stock Pitch Competition, requiring students to synthesize their training into a high-conviction investment thesis.
